Pho

Description

This is pho, the most well-known Vietnamese dish.

Ingredients

  • Steak
  • Thin rice noodles
  • Pho broth
  • Fresh herbs
  • Bean sprouts
  • Lime wedges

Steps

  1. Char the onions and ginger
  2. Make the broth
  3. Prep the noodles
  4. Assemble
  5. Serve immediately
